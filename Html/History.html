History


Vlux's history dates back to 1845, when the company initially specialized in the production of tortoiseshell spectacle frames. Over the years, it evolved, and by 1975, it established itself as a leader in providing cutting-edge weatherproof lighting solutions, renowned for both durability and innovation in the field.

With extensive expertise in various manufacturing techniques, such as injection molding, extrusion, thermoforming, and compression molding of glass-fiber reinforced polyester, Vlux controls the entire process from design to assembly and wiring. This holistic approach ensures the creation of world-class, reliable products.

Vlux differentiates itself in the market by producing its own high-quality glass-fiber reinforced polyester, which serves as the core material for many of its products.
